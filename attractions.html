Taniti has a large volcano located in the area that you can visit. It is an active volcano and it doesn't cost anything to visit this volcano. It is a beauty of nature that many people love to admire. It brings tourists back every year to visit this surreal experience.

The island has several beaches all over Taniti. The beaches are a great spot to enjoy the sun and swim in the Pacific ocean if you desire to do so. It's also great for surfing, scuba-diving, and more. The water is crystal clear and free of pollution while the white sand is soft as silk.

Fishing is another activity that people can participate in because it's a common thing to do in this island. If you enjoy eating freshly caught fish, then this experience is for you because you will be able to find an abundant amount of fish that can be caught in the waters near this island.

About us

Our website is geared towards providing valuable information about the island of Taniti. We want to ensure that you receive accurate information about this island. Tourism is what drives the economy of the island of Taniti because the economy was dependent upon fishing and agriculture prior to an increase in tourism. You will be able to find information on popular tourist attractions, transport, and lodging. You will have many opportunities to explore this island and enjoy the beauty of this geographic region. We encourage you to enjoy your stay at this island as we have developed our infrastructure for tourists to enjoy.

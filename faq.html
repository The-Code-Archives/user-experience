Frequently Asked Questions

What is the voltage of power sockets in Taniti?
The voltage is 120V which is the same type used in the US.
What is the legal drinking age in Taniti?
The legal drinking age in Taniti is 18.
Do they allow service for alcohol 24/7?
Alcohol is not allowed to be served or sold during midnight to 9 am.
What language do Tanitians speak?
Many younger Tanitians speak fluent English. Very little English is spoken in rural areas, especially by the older residents.
Are there hospitals or medical clinics in case of emergency?
There is one hospital and several health clinics. The employees are generally multilingual.
What is the violent crime rate like in Taniti?
Violent crime is very rare in Taniti, but as tourism increases, there are more reports of pickpocketing and petty theft.
Are there local holidays in Taniti?
Taniti enjoys a large number of national holidays, and many tourist attractions and restaurants will be closed on holidays, so visitors should plan accordingly.
What currency(s) is/are accepted in Taniti?
Taniti uses the U.S. dollar as its currency, but many businesses will also accept euros and yen. Several banks facilitate currency exchange, and many businesses accept major credit cards.
